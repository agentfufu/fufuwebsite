How To Earn Nano

But first, What is Nano?

Nano is a decentralized peer-to-peer digital currency. Nano was launched in October 2015 by Colin LeMahieu, with the aim of addressing blockchain scalability limitations that can result in restrictive fees and increased transaction confirmation times under load.

Nano Features

  • Fee-Less Transactions!
  • Eco-Friendly! Without relying on mining, printing or minting, Nano is sustainable digital money.
  • Instant Transactions! Nano is ready when you are.
